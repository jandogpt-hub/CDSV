## 10. DOMAIN TERMINOLOGY

### Technical Terms
- **SiO2** — Silicon Dioxide (ceramic coating ingredient)
- **Nano-ceramic** — Nanometer-scale particles for deep bonding
- **Hydrophobic** — Water-repelling property
- **Clear coat** — Transparent protective layer over base paint
- **Oxidation** — Chemical breakdown from oxygen exposure

### Process Terms
- **Paint correction** — Multi-stage polishing to remove defects
- **Decontamination** — Removal of embedded particles
- **Clay bar treatment** — Physical contaminant removal
- **Curing** — Chemical setting process for coatings

### Acronyms
- **PPF** — Paint Protection Film
- **UV** — Ultraviolet
- **RO** — Reverse Osmosis
- **DA** — Dual-Action (polisher)

### Key Specifications
- 3-year warranty (reactive polymer)
- 5-year warranty (nano ceramic)
- $1,000,000 liability insurance
- 16+ years experience
- Service areas: Tulare, Visalia, Hanford, Lemoore, Selma, Fresno

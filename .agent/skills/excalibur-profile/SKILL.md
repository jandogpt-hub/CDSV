# Excalibur Mobile Detail — Master Business Profile

## 1. Executive Summary

Excalibur Mobile Detail is a premier, concierge-level automotive preservation firm specializing in high-performance ceramic coatings and paint correction. Based in Visalia, California, and serving the greater Central Valley, the company is led by Art, a 16-year veteran in automotive surface science.

Excalibur operates on the **"Protective Guardian"** philosophy: treating every vehicle as a high-value financial asset that requires scientific-grade defense. By utilizing aviation-grade technology and offering official CarFax registration for its services, Excalibur provides a level of protection and value-retention that standard detailing shops cannot match.

## 5. Flagship Product Catalog (Overview)

### Ceramic Coating Tiers
1. **Sapphire V1 / Onyx (7–10 Year Protection):** Our flagship Aviation-Grade coating. Extreme chemical resistance and permanent bonding. Includes official CarFax Registration.
2. **Quartz+ Series (5 Year Protection):** The ideal balance of high-gloss aesthetics and long-term durability for daily drivers.
3. **Quartz Series (3 Year Protection):** Entry-level nanotechnology that makes waxing obsolete.

### Surface Restoration
- **Stage 2 Paint Correction:** A meticulous machine-polishing process to remove swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation before coating.
- **GlassParency Application:** Advanced hydrophobic glass treatment to improve safety and visibility in Central Valley weather.

### The Car Care Program
- A recurring, subscription-based maintenance service designed to preserve the integrity of the ceramic coating and ensure the vehicle remains in "Showroom Condition" year-round.

## 6. Competitive Edge (USP)

- **CarFax Integration:** We are the only local mobile provider that turns a detail into a documented financial asset by registering the coating on the vehicle's permanent history report.
- **Aviation Pedigree:** Using Flight Shield technology ensures our coatings can withstand the same environmental extremes faced by private jets.
- **Full Autonomy:** Our mobile units are self-contained with specialized Reverse Osmosis (RO) Water, ensuring a spot-free finish even in areas with hard water.
